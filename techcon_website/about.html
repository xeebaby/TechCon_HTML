History

TechCon began in 2015 as a small regional developer meetup in Austin, Texas, founded by a group of passionate engineers who envisioned a space where tech enthusiasts could share ideas, collaborate, and inspire one another. The first event welcomed just 150 attendees, featuring grassroots workshops and open-source showcases. Over the years, TechCon has evolved into a global stage for innovation, drawing thousands of participants from across the world. Each year has marked a milestone — from introducing live-streamed keynotes in 2017 to launching the Women in Tech Summit in 2019 and going fully hybrid during the 2020 pandemic. Today, TechCon stands as a beacon for the tech community, spotlighting breakthroughs in AI, Web3, sustainability, and digital transformation. Historical images from past events — from humble classroom-style beginnings to today's packed auditoriums and cutting-edge expo halls — reflect the journey of how a simple idea grew into one of the most anticipated tech events of the year.
